What is Food Carbon Footprint (fcf) ?

The food we consume contributes to climate change. The production, packaging and transportation of food all consumes energy and results in carbon emissions which threaten to raise average global surface temperatures.

However, not all foods are equal...

The Food Carbon Footprint Tracker (FCFT) provides the opportunity to track the resultant carbon dioxide from the food you eat, called your "Food Carbon Footprint".

This website also offers personalised and practical ways to reduce the carbon footprint of your diet, reducing your impact on climate change.
